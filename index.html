Who we are

Bloomberg New Economy is the worldwide platform for dialogue and discourse about economic transformation in growth markets.We convene leaders from across government and private enterprise to confront the greatest challenges to global prosperity and to collaborate on solutions. We develop agenda-setting programs to explore the forces driving change across finance, trade and industry. We build coalitions of partners committed to scaling innovation and mobilizing capital for public good.

This initiative has nurtured a community of current and former heads of state, senior ministers, heads of multilateral organizations, multinational CEOs, founders, board chairs, investors, financiers, innovators and activists.

Bloomberg New Economy 2025

Bloomberg New Economy is the worldwide platform for dialogue and discourse about economic transformation in growth markets. We convene leaders from across government and private enterprise to confront the greatest challenges to global prosperity and to collaborate on solutions. We develop agenda-setting programs to explore the forces driving change across finance, trade and industry. We build coalitions of partners committed to scaling innovation and mobilizing capital for public good.

This initiative has nurtured a community of current and former heads of state, senior ministers, heads of multilateral organizations, multinational CEOs, founders, board chairs, investors, financiers, innovators and activists. Join us on Nov. 19-21, 2025, as our flagship New Economy Forum returns to Singapore for three days of plenary sessions and discussion groups under our guiding theme, Thriving in an Age of Extremes, as well as unparalleled access and relationship-building opportunities.
